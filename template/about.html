About Us

  Pizza is all about quality you can trust. As one of the original founding

 Our classic pan pizza will always be a fan favourite, with a soft and 
    chewy crust perfectly balancing out the healthy tomato pure

Our authentic Italian crust for those who would prefer a light and 
airy crust to more fully enjoy the toppings. Thin, light
